COLLEGE OF EDUCATION

This platform is created to support Literacy Training Service (LTS) students in their academic growth and community engagement journey. It provides well-organized learning materials and resources that aim to enhance teaching competencies, improve communication and literacy skills, and promote a strong sense of social responsibility. Through guided lessons, activities, and references, students are equipped with the knowledge and tools needed to become effective educators and community facilitators.

Through these resources, students are encouraged to apply what they have learned in real-life situations, particularly in community-based and outreach programs. The platform serves as a learning space where students can reflect on their experiences, develop compassion, and strengthen their commitment to service. This website acts as a guide for LTS students as they prepare to become responsible, socially aware, and service-oriented individuals who contribute meaningfully to community development.
